Abstract

My name is Sriharsha Kocherla, and my research interests lie in quantum algorithms and machine learning.

I am currently working at the Computational Physics lab at Georgia Tech, where I focus on developing quantum algorithms for modeling fluid flows. I've been in this position since September 2021.

Prior to this, I was involved in ML research and contributed to the development of a model that detects retinal junctions.

Overall, I'm passionate about developing new computational approaches and am excited to continue exploring these fields.
